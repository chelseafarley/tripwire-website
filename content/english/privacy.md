## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how this app handles your information.

## Information We Collect

The App does **not** collect, transmit, or sell personal information.

All information you enter into the App is stored **locally on your device** using an SQLite database.

## Local Storage

Data remains on your device unless you choose to delete the App or clear its data. The developer does not have access to this information.

## Analytics, Tracking, and Advertising

The App:

- Does not use analytics.
- Does not use advertising.
- Does not use tracking technologies.
- Does not share data with third parties.

## Permissions

The App only requests permissions required for its functionality. If additional permissions are added in future releases, this policy will be updated.

## Data Security

Because your data is stored only on your device, you are responsible for protecting access to your device.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer using the contact details provided on the app store listing or within the App. Emails can be directed to [chelsea@tripwiretech.com](mailto:chelsea@tripwiretech.com).
